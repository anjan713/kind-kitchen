# Product Brief

## Context

KindKitchen is a proof-of-concept recipe adapter for a parent using a phone in a hospital or care setting. The parent may have limited time and unreliable bandwidth. The application must offer a practical recipe adjustment without pretending to calculate exact nutrition or make a clinical decision.

## Scenario

The recipe is **Creamy Tomato Soup**. The saved demo profile is **fat-free** and **low-sodium**. The parent can tap a flagged ingredient or ask for one by name.

## Desired outcome

The parent should see the original recipe first, understand the scan labels, apply exactly one resolved swap at a time, see matching steps update instantly, hear the same updated text if narration is active, and undo when needed. The demo must remain usable without a service, AI, microphone, or account.

## Non-goals

This is not a clinical nutrition calculator, medical recommendation engine, product barcode system, persistent recipe planner, or account product.
